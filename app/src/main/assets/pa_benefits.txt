The benefits of exercising regularly include a lower risk of developing many serious diseases, including:
    -  Heart disease
    -  Stroke
    -  Diabetes
    -  Some types of cancer
    -  Dementia
    -  Depression

Exercise can also help with your OA symptoms. And, if you're trying to lose weight, exercise can help with this too.
